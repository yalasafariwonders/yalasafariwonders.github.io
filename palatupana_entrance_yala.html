Palatupana Entrance: The main entrance of Yala National Park

Palatupana is the most popular entrance to Yala National Park, a renowned wildlife sanctuary in Sri Lanka. Located near the town of Kirinda, this gateway offers visitors a direct route into the park's Block 1.

The convenience of its location near Kirinda and Tissamaharama, coupled with its proximity to the park's most sought-after block, makes Palatupana a popular choice for visitors.

Palatupana is situated approximately 13 kilometers from the town of Kirinda and 22 kilometers from Tissamaharama.

Opening and Closing Times: The park typically opens at 6:00 AM to 12:00 PM and 2:00 PM to 6:00 PM. However, these timings may vary slightly depending on the season.

Park Ticket Reservation: While advance reservations are not mandatory, it's recommended to book your jeep safari in advance, especially during peak seasons. This ensures availability and helps you plan your visit efficiently.
